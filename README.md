# EcoTrack AI 🌱

EcoTrack AI is a web-based Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform designed to help individuals understand, monitor, and reduce their environmental impact. The platform calculates carbon emissions generated through daily activities such as transportation, electricity consumption, food habits, and waste generation.

Users can visualize their environmental impact through interactive dashboards, track sustainability goals, and receive personalized recommendations for reducing their carbon footprint. The platform promotes eco-friendly decision-making and encourages sustainable living through data-driven insights.

## Key Features

* Carbon Footprint Calculator
* Transportation Emission Tracking
* Electricity Usage Analysis
* Food Consumption Impact Assessment
* Waste Management Tracking
* Interactive Analytics Dashboard
* Sustainability Score Monitoring
* Personalized Eco-Friendly Recommendations
* Goal Setting and Progress Tracking
* Responsive and Modern User Interface

## Technology Stack
